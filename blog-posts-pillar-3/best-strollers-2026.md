## What Makes a Great Stroller in 2026?

Modern strollers have evolved dramatically. Here's what top-rated models offer:

- **Storage**: Large baskets, cup holders, parent organizers
- **Longevity**: From newborn to toddler (0-50 lbs typically)
- **Easy Cleaning**: Removable, machine-washable fabrics

## How to Choose the Right Stroller

### Consider Your Lifestyle

**City Living**:
- Prioritize: Lightweight, compact fold, maneuverability
- Look for: Babyzen YOYO2, UPPAbaby MINU
- Avoid: Wide joggers, bulky travel systems

**Suburban Life**:
- Prioritize: Storage, comfort, versatility
- Look for: UPPAbaby Vista, Graco Modes
- Avoid: Ultra-minimalist models

**Active Parents (Runners/Hikers)**:
- Prioritize: Suspension, sturdy wheels, hand brake
- Look for: BOB Revolution, Thule Urban Glide
- Avoid: Lightweight city strollers

**Frequent Travelers**:
- Prioritize: Compact fold, light weight, car seat compatibility
- Look for: Babyzen YOYO2, Chicco Bravo
- Avoid: Heavy travel systems

## Common Stroller Mistakes to Avoid

**1. Buying Before Baby Arrives**
Wait until you understand your routine. You might not need a stroller immediately if you babywear.

**2. Choosing Fashion Over Function**
That Instagram-worthy stroller might not fit your lifestyle. Prioritize features over aesthetics.

**3. Ignoring Weight and Fold**
If you live in a walk-up or have a small car trunk, a heavy or bulky stroller becomes a daily burden.

**4. Skipping the Test Drive**
Always test fold, unfold, and steer before buying. What seems easy in videos might frustrate you in real life.

**5. Not Planning for Growth**
Babies grow fast. Ensure your stroller works from newborn through toddler years (or plan for two separate strollers).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's the best stroller for a newborn?
Look for models with full recline (180 degrees) or bassinet mode. The UPPAbaby Vista, Graco Modes Pramette, and Bugaboo Fox all offer newborn-safe configurations from day one.

### Do I really need an expensive stroller?
No! Great strollers exist at every price point. The Baby Trend Expedition ($89) and Graco Modes ($279) offer excellent features. Expensive models provide better materials, smoother rides, and longer warranties, but aren't essential.

### How long will I use a stroller?
Most parents use strollers until age 3-4, though some continue until age 5. Choose a model rated for at least 50 lbs to maximize longevity.

### Can I use a jogging stroller for everyday use?
Yes, but they're bulkier and harder to maneuver in tight spaces (stores, restaurants). If you run 2+ times weekly, a dedicated jogger is worth it. Otherwise, choose a standard stroller.

### What's the difference between a stroller and a travel system?
A travel system includes a stroller and infant car seat that clicks into the frame. This lets you move sleeping babies from car to stroller without waking them. Great for newborns, less useful after 6 months.

### Is a double stroller worth it for twins or close-aged siblings?
Absolutely for twins. For siblings 2+ years apart, consider a ride-along board attachment for your single stroller instead—much cheaper and more versatile.

### How do I clean a stroller?
Most fabrics are machine washable (check your manual). For quick cleaning: wipe frames with baby-safe wipes, vacuum the seat, spot-clean with mild soap and water. Deep clean every 3 months.

### Should I buy new or used?
Used strollers can save 50-70% and many are barely used. Check for recalls, test all functions, and clean thoroughly. Avoid used car seats but strollers are usually safe secondhand.
