## Target Market Analysis

### Primary Audience
- **Demographics**: Expectant parents (25-40 years old)
- **Psychographics**: Seeking meaningful, unique names
- **Pain Points**:
  - Overwhelmed by too many options
  - Partner disagreement on names
  - Want cultural significance
  - Fear of name being too common/too unique

### Search Intent Categories
1. **Informational** (60%): "what does [name] mean", "origin of [name]"
2. **Navigational** (15%): "baby name app", "name finder"
3. **Commercial** (20%): "best baby names 2024", "unique baby names"
4. **Transactional** (5%): "baby name generator", "name tool"
