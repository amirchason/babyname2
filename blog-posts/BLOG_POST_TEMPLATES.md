## WRITING TIPS FOR ALL POSTS

### SEO Optimization Checklist:
- [ ] Target keyword in: Title, first paragraph, H1, URL, meta description
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H2 headings
- [ ] 15-20 internal links to name pages and other blog posts
- [ ] 3-5 external links to authoritative sources (SSA, etymology dictionaries)
- [ ] Alt text for any images
- [ ] Schema.org Article markup at bottom
- [ ] Meta description under 155 characters
- [ ] Engaging CTA at end

### Content Quality Checklist:
- [ ] 2,000+ words (longer for high-competition keywords)
- [ ] Scannable format (short paragraphs, bullet points, headings)
- [ ] Conversational, helpful tone (write for parents, not robots)
- [ ] Accurate information (verify meanings and origins)
- [ ] FAQ section (answers "People Also Ask" from Google)
- [ ] Unique content (never copy from competitors)
- [ ] Proofread (use Grammarly or similar)

### After Publishing:
- [ ] Submit URL to Google Search Console for indexing
- [ ] Share on social media (with custom og:image)
- [ ] Add to sitemap
- [ ] Create internal links from other blog posts
- [ ] Monitor rankings in GSC after 2-3 weeks
- [ ] Update every 6 months to keep fresh
