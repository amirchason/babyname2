## The Art of the Preppy Middle Name

Now, let's talk about middle names because they're just as important as first names! The beauty of choosing preppy baby names lies in how they harmonize with middle names—even if you go for something traditional or quirky!

#### Tips for Choosing That Perfect Middle Name:

- Combine Classic & Trendy: If you've chosen a strong first name like Charlotte, consider pairing it with something spunky like Sloane.
- Family Heirlooms: Using grandma’s maiden name or dad's favorite uncle can create sentimental value.
- Flow Matters: Say them aloud together; does Charlotte Waverly roll off the tongue smoothly?
- Initials are Key: Avoid combinations that spell embarrassing acronyms!
