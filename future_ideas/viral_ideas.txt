## Social/Competitive Features

### Partner Mode (HIGH PRIORITY)
Both parents swipe separately, app reveals only mutual likes (like Tinder for baby names).
- Creates tension + shareable "we matched!" moments
- Organic word-of-mouth growth: "you NEED to download this with your partner"
- Potential for viral social media shares of matched results

### Name Battles
Daily bracket-style voting on name matchups.
- Leaderboard shows trending names = FOMO
- Daily engagement hook
- Community-driven content

### Roast My Name List
AI generates funny roasts of users' favorite names.
- Shareable to TikTok/Instagram
- Humor = viral potential
- User-generated content opportunities

### Regret Wall
Crowdsourced submissions of "names parents regret" with funny stories.
- User-generated content
- Highly relatable = shareability
- Community engagement

## Viral Content Generators

### Name Personality Quiz
"Your baby name taste reveals you're a..." personality quiz format.
- Highly shareable format (proven viral mechanic)
- Low barrier to entry
- Easy social media integration

### Celebrity Baby Name Generator
AI suggests names based on celebrity baby naming patterns.
- Pop culture angle = engagement
- Shareable results
