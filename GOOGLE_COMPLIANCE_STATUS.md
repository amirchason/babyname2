### GDPR Requirements:
- Privacy Policy is MANDATORY (we collect user data)
- Cookie consent banner required for EU users
- Users must have right to access, delete, export data
- Clear disclosure of third-party services (Google, Firebase)
- Data retention policies must be specified

- "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link required
- Privacy Policy must list data categories collected
- Must honor opt-out requests within 15 days
- Fines up to $7,988 per intentional violation

### Google Search Requirements:
- Sitemap.xml for efficient crawling
- robots.txt to guide crawlers
- Structured data for rich snippets
- Mobile-first indexing compliance
- Core Web Vitals optimization
