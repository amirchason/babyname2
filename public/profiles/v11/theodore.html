The Story Behind the Name

Theodore has roots deeply planted in the Greek language, where it translates to 'Gift of God.' This lovely sentiment is captured in the original Greek as 'Theodoros,' a combination of 'theos,' meaning God, and 'doron,' meaning gift. Isn't it just poetic to think of each child as a divine gift, a cherished blessing sent to brighten our lives?

Over the centuries, Theodore has traveled through time and across borders, settling into various cultures and languages while maintaining its core meaning. Today, it carries the same dignified grace, resonating with an acknowledgment of life's beautiful surprises. It's a name that continues to celebrate the joy and wonder of new life, a nod to divine generosity that feels as fresh now as it ever did.
