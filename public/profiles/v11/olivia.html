The name Olivia has its roots firmly planted in Latin soil, where it translates to 'olive tree.' Historically, the olive tree has been a symbol of harmony and prosperity, making Olivia a name that resonates with positive vibrations and promise.

Delving into its journey through time, Olivia first truly blossomed in the literary world thanks to William Shakespeare's 'Twelfth Night.'
